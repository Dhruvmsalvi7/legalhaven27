Our Story

Built To Level The Playing Field.

Legal Haven exists to ensure that no one is denied justice because of income, language, or circumstance. We are a student-led team working alongside legal professionals.

Who We Are

  • Texas based nonprofit model focused on legal access and education
  • Founded to bridge vulnerable communities with trusted legal pathways
  • Supported by student volunteers, advisors, and partner organizations
  • Committed to culturally aware, trauma-informed support
"Justice is strongest when support is local, understandable, and human."

Who We Serve

People Facing High Stakes Problems

We focus on individuals and families facing urgent legal needs where early guidance can prevent crisis.

Low Income Households

Families at risk of eviction, debt actions, or benefit loss.

Immigrant Families

Support navigating documentation and local service referrals.

Seniors

Guidance on housing, caregiving, fraud, and civil protections.

Students And Workers

Rights education and next-step planning for workplace or school disputes.

Our Principles

How We Show Up For Our Community

Accessible Language

Dignity First

Action Oriented
