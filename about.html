Our Story

Built To Level The Playing Field.

Legal Haven exists to ensure that no one is denied justice because of income, language, or circumstance. We are a student-led team working alongside legal professionals.

Mission Statement

Justice should never depend on zip code, income, or background.

LegalHaven is committed to helping underserved communities find legal pathways with dignity, clarity, and practical support.

Leadership

The Co-Founders Of LegalHaven

Dhruv Salvi, President & Co-Founder

Dhruv is a Political Science & Government student at Rutgers University who co-founded LegalHaven with the belief that no one should be turned away from help because of where they are from, their income, or their story. As President, they lead the organization's overall vision, strategy, and operations, and serve as LegalHaven's primary voice to partners, donors, and the communities it serves.

Vianica Villarin, Vice President & Co-Founder

Vianica is a Political Science (Legal Emphasis) student at Brigham Young University who co-founded LegalHaven out of a commitment to help create bridges between marginalized and underserved communities to legal resources as they often cannot seek help. As Vice President, they oversee day-to-day programming and outreach, build partnerships, and work to ensure LegalHaven's resources reach the people who need them most.

Dhiraj Angadipeta, Treasurer & Co-Founder

Dhiraj is a Finance student at The University of Texas at Dallas who co-founded the organization with a commitment to expanding access to legal help for those who need it most. As Treasurer, they oversee budgeting, financial operations, and long-term planning to ensure sustainability and maximize the nonprofit's impact.

Who We Serve

  • Low-income households navigating housing, debt, and benefit challenges
  • Immigrant families seeking trusted legal direction and referrals
  • Seniors facing fraud, caregiving, and civil protection concerns
  • Students and workers who need rights education and legal resources
