Services

Practical Legal Support, Built Around Real Life.

Our services are designed to lower confusion, reduce risk, and connect people to reliable legal pathways quickly.

Core Service Areas

Legal Triage

Fast issue screening to identify urgency, timelines, and immediate protections.

Document Support

Assistance organizing key records before consultation or clinic appointments.

Referral Matching

Connections to legal aid, pro bono teams, and trusted partner organizations.

Housing Guidance

Support for tenants navigating eviction risk, notices, and local protections.

Family Safety Planning

Help finding safe legal options for custody, protective orders, and stability needs.

Community Clinics

In-person and pop-up sessions in schools, churches, and neighborhood centers.

What We Do Not Provide

Legal Haven does not replace licensed attorney representation. We focus on navigation, education, and referrals to proper legal channels.

Response Expectations

Intake requests are reviewed in 1 to 2 business days. Emergency or time-sensitive issues should also be directed to local legal aid hotlines.
